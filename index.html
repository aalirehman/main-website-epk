artist biography

Los Angeles-based producer and DJ Aali Rehman blends dub textures with hypnotic techno, anchored by the driving rhythms of minimal techno.

His productions pull from deep hypnotic grooves, atmospheric sound design, and stripped-back percussion, creating immersive sets and releases built for late-night dancefloors. He has shared the stage with Matador, Jonas Saalbach, Robag Wruhme, Theus Mago, Raxon, Henry Saiz, Nicole Moudaber, TDJ, Cristoph, Lindsey Herbert, Etari, Cassie Raptor, Mesme, Marie Nyx, Materielle, Wes Mills, Caravel, and more.
