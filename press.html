artist biography

Los Angeles-based producer and DJ Aali Rehman blends dub textures with hypnotic techno, anchored by the driving rhythms of minimal techno

His productions draw from a number of influences, weaving together elements that feel both experimental and accessible. He has shared the stage with Matador, Jonas Saalbach, Robag Wruhme, Theus Mago, Raxon, Henry Saiz, Nicole Moudaber, TDJ, Cristoph, Lindsey Herbert, Etari, Cassie Raptor, Mesme, Marie Nyx, Materielle, Wes Mills, Caravel, and more.
